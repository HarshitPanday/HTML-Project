"Jhunjhunwala Group of Institutions" have evolved and adopted the teaching programs and methodology requiring the students to undergo the rigor of the professional world in form as well as in substance providing them an opportunity to apply their classroom knowledge, thus as build the long needed bridge between the professional world and the educational world. The Institutions wants its Degree, Diploma, BCA, ITI and Medical students to imbibe three key values: the intellectual curiosity to inquire, the personal capabilities to inspire and to show the commitment to deliver positive social impact. These values have been made practical by involving industry in curriculum design, internships and placements and by engaging student minds with social purpose through research and hands-on projects. Consequently students will spend between 20 to 30% of their total internships during their course duration.

Welcome to Jhunjhunwala Medical College

Admissions

Admissions to Jhunjhunwala Medical College are highly competitive. Prospective students are selected based on academic merit, entrance exam scores, and personal interviews. We seek candidates who demonstrate a passion for medicine, a commitment to service, and the potential to become future leaders in healthcare.

At Jhunjhunwala Medical College, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of medical professionals who will serve with excellence, compassion, and integrity. Our institution stands as a beacon of quality medical education, dedicated to advancing healthcare through education, research, and clinical practice.

Our Mission

  1. To provide a comprehensive and innovative medical education that fosters critical thinking, lifelong learning, and ethical practice.
  2. To cultivate a culture of research and discovery that contributes to advancements in medical science and improves patient care.
  3. To promote community engagement and service, addressing the healthcare needs of our local and global communities.

Why Choose Jhunjhunwala Medical College?

  1. Expert Faculty: Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, committed to mentoring and guiding students to achieve their highest potential.
  2. State-of-the-Art Facilities: From modern laboratories to advanced simulation centers, we provide the resources students need to excel in their studies and clinical training.
  3. Clinical Exposure:Through partnerships with renowned hospitals and healthcare institutions, students gain hands-on experience and exposure to diverse medical specialties.
  4. Research Opportunities:Students have access to cutting-edge research facilities and opportunities to collaborate with faculty on groundbreaking research projects.
  5. Global Perspective: We embrace diversity and offer opportunities for international exchanges and collaborations, preparing students to thrive in a global healthcare environment.

Academic Programs

  • Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)
  • Doctor of Medicine (MD) in various specialties
  • Master of Surgery (MS) in various specialties
  • Doctorate of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Medical Sciences
